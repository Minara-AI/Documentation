# Trade assets

Minara supports trading both crypto tokens and tokenized stocks using the same USDC-based flow. This page covers how to buy and sell both asset types.

{% hint style="info" %}
USDC is the base currency for all trades. If you haven't deposited yet, see [how-to-deposit-funds.md](how-to-deposit-funds.md "mention").
{% endhint %}

## Cryptocurrency

### Via token detail

**1. Log in to Minara**

Visit Minara and log in to your account.

**2. Open token detail**

In the token list sidebar (which appears when tokens are mentioned in the chat), select the token you want to trade and click **"View Detail."**

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/图片 (21).png" alt="Token list sidebar with View Detail button highlighted"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**3. Set up your trade**

Enter the amount you wish to trade. Unfold the "Receive Amount" section to review estimated fees. You can also adjust slippage tolerance in settings.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/图片 (23).png" alt="Trade setup panel showing amount input and fee breakdown"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**4. Confirm and execute**

Review the estimated rate and network fees, then click **"Buy"** to execute.

> _The same process applies to "Sell."_

### Via prompt

**1. Log in to Minara**

Visit Minara and log in to your account.

**2. Type your request in the chat**

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/img_v3_02ot_594d701d-97b1-4dce-bcbd-b9361f8f4aag.png" alt="Chat prompt for buying a crypto token"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**3. Confirm with one click**

Minara generates an interactive plugin in her response. Click **"Yes"** to proceed — no extra setup required.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/图片 (24).png" alt="Confirmation plugin with Yes button"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Tokenized stocks

Minara supports tokenized stocks — digital versions of company shares that track real stock prices on-chain.

{% hint style="info" %}
A **tokenized stock** mirrors a real stock's market price and typically pays dividends, but does not carry voting rights.
{% endhint %}

### Via token detail

**1. Log in to Minara**

Visit Minara and log in to your account.

**2. Open token detail**

Search for a stock token directly to trade it, or search for a traditional stock ticker — Minara will guide you to its on-chain tokenized version.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/图片 (17).png" alt="Token list sidebar showing a stock token"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/图片 (25).png" alt="Minara guiding from traditional stock search to tokenized version"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**3. Set up your trade**

Enter the amount. Unfold "Receive Amount" for fee details. Adjust slippage in settings if needed.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/图片 (18).png" alt="Trade setup panel for stock token"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**4. Confirm and execute**

Review the rate and fees, then click **"Buy"** to execute.

> _The same process applies to "Sell."_

### Via prompt

**1. Log in to Minara**

Visit Minara and log in to your account.

**2. Type your request in the chat**

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/图片 (19).png" alt="Chat prompt for buying a stock token"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**3. Confirm with one click**

Minara generates an interactive plugin in her response. Click **"Yes"** to proceed.
