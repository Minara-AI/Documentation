# Subscription & top-up policy

***

## Plans

| Plan | Monthly credits | Workflows | Extras |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Lite** | 1,400 | 5 | — |
| **Starter** | 4,000 | 20 | — |
| **Pro** | 20,000 | 50 | API Access |

All plans include unlimited Autopilot and unlimited Copilot execution.

Credits reset at the start of each billing cycle. Unused credits do not carry over to the next cycle.

***

## Billing

- Subscription fees are charged upfront and are non-refundable unless required by law or explicitly stated in a promotion.
- Subscriptions renew automatically at the end of each billing cycle unless you downgrade to the free plan.

***

## Changing plans

### Upgrading

Upgrading switches you from a lower-tier plan to a higher-tier one.

- Takes effect immediately after payment
- Your billing cycle renewal date stays the same
- You are charged the price difference between the new plan and your current plan
- Credits from the new plan's allocation are added immediately, with the same expiry date

**Example:**

> You subscribed to the Starter Plan ($49) on May 1. On May 10, you upgrade to the Partner Plan ($199). You are charged $199 − $49 = $150. Your plan upgrades immediately and still renews on June 1.

### Downgrading

Downgrading switches you from a higher-tier plan to a lower-tier one.

- Takes effect at the start of the next billing cycle
- Your current plan remains active until the renewal date

### Cross-plan switching rules

| From \ To | Plan tier |
| --- | --- |
| Monthly → Monthly | Any plan |
| Yearly → Yearly | Any plan |
| Monthly → Yearly | Equivalent or higher |
| Yearly → Monthly | Not yet supported |

***

## Top-up credit packs

### When you can purchase

- You must be on a paid subscription plan
- Your current subscription credits must be fully consumed

### How top-up credits work

1. Top-up credits are only available while an active paid subscription is in place
2. Top-up credits are used only after your subscription credits run out
3. Top-up credits are supplementary — they do not replace subscription credits

**Example scenarios:**

- June 28: your subscription credits run out, you buy a top-up. You are now drawing from top-up credits.
- June 29: your subscription expires. You are now on the free plan. Top-up credits are unavailable until you reactivate a paid subscription.
- July 1: you resubscribe. Your top-up credits are preserved and will be used after the new plan's credits are exhausted.

***

## What stops when credits run out

When your subscription credits are fully consumed, the following features pause until credits are restored:

- Chatting with Minara
- Deep Research
- Creating new workflows
- Running workflows that include Minara Call nodes

Autopilot and Copilot execution are not affected by credit balance.

***

## Refund policy

All subscription fees and top-up purchases are non-refundable after payment, except where required by applicable consumer protection laws or explicitly outlined in a special offer.

No refunds are issued for:

- Downgrades
- Mid-cycle cancellations
- Expired plans
- Unused top-up credits
