A **tokenized stock** is a digital version of a company's stock on a blockchain. Its value mirrors the real stock's market price, and dividends are typically paid. Unlike traditional shares, most tokens do not carry voting rights.

## Via token detail

**1. Log in to Minara**

Visit Minara and log in to your account.

**2. Open token detail**

Search for a stock token directly to trade it, or search for a traditional stock ticker — Minara will guide you to its on-chain tokenized version.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/图片 (17).png" alt="Token list sidebar showing a stock token"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/图片 (25).png" alt="Minara guiding from traditional stock search to tokenized version"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**3. Set up your trade**

Enter the amount. Unfold "Receive Amount" for fee details. Adjust slippage in settings if needed.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/图片 (18).png" alt="Trade setup panel for stock token"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**4. Confirm and execute**

Review the rate and fees, then click **"Buy"** to execute.

> _The same process applies to "Sell."_
