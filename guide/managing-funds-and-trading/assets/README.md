# Assets

Minara lets you trade three categories of assets from a single account, using USDC as the base currency.

**Crypto tokens** — spot swaps across thousands of tokens on EVM chains and Solana. Buy, sell, or ask Minara to execute trades via chat.

**Tokenized stocks** — digital versions of company shares that track real stock prices on-chain. Supports major US equities. Dividends are typically paid; voting rights are not included.

**Commodities** — tokenized commodities such as gold (XAUUSDC) traded as perpetuals.

See the pages below for step-by-step guides on buying and selling each asset type.
