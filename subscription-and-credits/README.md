# Subscription & Credits

Minara uses a credit system to meter AI usage. Credits are consumed by AI queries — chat messages, Deep Research reports, and workflow nodes that invoke Minara's AI. Strategy execution through Autopilot and Copilot position management do not consume credits.

All plans include unlimited Autopilot and unlimited Copilot. Credits determine how much you can query the AI, not how much you can trade.

## Plans

| Plan | Monthly credits | Workflows | Extras |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Lite** | 1,400 | 5 | — |
| **Starter** | 4,000 | 20 | — |
| **Pro** | 20,000 | 50 | API Access |

Credits reset at the start of each billing cycle. If you run out, you can purchase a top-up credit pack (available on paid plans only) or upgrade to a higher plan, which takes effect immediately.

For details on how credits are consumed, see [How Credits Work](how-credits-work.md). For billing rules, upgrades, and top-ups, see [Subscription & Top-up Policy](subscription-and-top-up-policy.md).
